### Thesis

The Zdouc Lab believes in short, but well-crafted Master theses.
In general, these should follow the classical paper-format (Abstract, Introduction, Results, Discussion, Methods, References).

Usually, they are less than 25 pages, but they can be longer, especially if there are many figures.

Theses should follow the regulation of the University of Vienna.
